A desktop app that runs powerful AI on your computer. No cloud. No telemetry. No account required. This 15-minute walkthrough gets you hands-on and lets us know what works.

1 Download for your system

Pick your platform and download the installer. File sizes are small (~10MB) — the AI models come later, inside the app.

macOS
Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3)
Download DMG
Windows
64-bit · 10 or later
Download EXE
Linux
Debian / Ubuntu x64
Download DEB
First-time security warning? macOS and Windows will show a warning because the app isn't code-signed yet. On Mac: right-click the app → Open → "Open anyway". On Windows: click "More info" → "Run anyway". This is safe — it's just the OS asking because we're a small publisher.

2 Run the 8-step smoke test

Spend about 15 minutes walking through the app. Note anything that doesn't work, looks wrong, or feels confusing.

1
Launch the app
Open the installed Strive Private AI app. You'll see a boot animation briefly.
Sidebar with 4 nav items, clean dark UI with cyan accents, Chat tab active
2
Navigate all 4 tabs
Click: Chat → Documents → Models → Settings. Each should load without errors.
Each tab loads in under 1 second, active item highlighted in cyan
3
Download your first model
Go to Models tab. Find Phi-3 Mini (~2GB). Click Download.
Progress bar animates 0 → 100% over 1–3 minutes (depends on internet)
4
Activate the model
Once downloaded, click the model → click Use (or "Load into memory").
Button changes to "Active" with a green indicator
5
Have a conversation
Click Chat tab. Type: Explain quantum computing in one paragraph
"Thinking locally…" indicator, then a response appears within 5–15 seconds
6
Upload a document
Click Documents → Add documents. Pick any small PDF or text file.
Native file picker opens. After selection, a success toast appears and the file shows in the list.
7
Chat with your document
Go back to Chat. Click the RAG chip (turns cyan). Ask something specific about your uploaded file.
Response references the content of your document
8
Check system info
Settings tab → About. Your real RAM, CPU cores, and OS should be shown.
Accurate hardware info (matches your Mac/PC specs)

3 What to watch for

✅ Report as "working"

  • Buttons respond when clicked
  • Downloads complete
  • AI responds to questions with text
  • File uploads succeed
  • No blank screens or frozen UI

❌ Report as a bug

  • Blank or dark screen that never loads
  • App crashes / quits unexpectedly
  • A click does nothing visible
  • Error message in a red toast
  • Download progress freezes
  • AI gives no response or errors
  • Text overlapping or layout looks broken
Privacy test (optional) Before step 5, disconnect your Wi-Fi. The chat should still work — that's the whole point. Reconnect before step 3 (you need internet to download the model initially).
